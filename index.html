What This Website Covers

This website supports future students preparing for the Computer Science Foundation Year. We will be focusing on one core Computational Thinking topic: Algorithms.

You will learn how algorithms solve problems through clear, step-by-step logic. Start with the theory, study worked examples, and then test your understanding with practice questions.
